Help the sanctuary

Homeless cats and dogs in Palawan, Philippines are always in need of help - animal feed, medicine, ammunition, transportation, financial assistance, and volunteers.

How can you help animals?

  • PR:

    You can help us with the dissemination of information about the sanctuary / animals and this site on the Internet, in the press, print and paste posters in the center, place an ad on your page or in a group on social networks, on a thematic forum, etc.

    We really need any informational support - the more people learn about what wonderful pets are waiting for them in the sanctuary, the more dogs and cats will become happy.

    call us

  • financially:

    At Street Dog Sanctuary, we rely heavily on donations to maintain our operations and help new animals get to the sanctuary. Every month, we spend $5000USD and growing on food alone. Every month, our medical costs range from $1500-$2500, depending on the dogs we rescue and maintaining our current medical at the sanctuary. Therefore, we will be grateful for any financial assistance.
